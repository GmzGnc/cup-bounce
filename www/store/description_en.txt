🎯 WHAT IS CUP BOUNCE?

Cup Bounce is a fast-paced arcade physics game where you use a slingshot to launch balls into cups. Pull back, find the perfect angle, and send the ball flying right into the target!

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

⚙️ HOW TO PLAY

🔹 Drag down on the slingshot to pull it back
🔹 Release at the right angle and power
🔹 The ball flies with real physics — gravity, bounce, arc!
🔹 Fill all the cups to advance to the next level
🔹 Shoot fast for score multipliers!
